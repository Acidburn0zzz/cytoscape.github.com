About the award

A cash prize was awarded to the students who have demonstrated excellence in Cytoscape-related development selected from the 2013 Google Summer of Code student pool. The winning students were announced at the Cytoscape Symposium and Workshop in Paris, France on October 9-10, 2013. Selection took into consideration the many aspects of Allan's perpective and contributions to Cytoscape and software development, including his passions for:

  • Mentoring software developers
  • Open source code
  • Good user-centered design and execution
  • Network biology tools/resources/applications
  • Cytoscape core and app development
  • Google Summer of Code

In dedication to

Allan J. Kuchinsky was a member of Agilent Labs working in molecular data analysis. His 35-year career at Agilent Technologies and Hewlett Packard included work in data visualization, human-computer interaction and computational biology. His expertise led him to the Cytoscape community, which he joined in 2005. He was a tireless champion for Cytoscape and open source software at Agilent and was a significant thought leader in the Cytoscape project.
